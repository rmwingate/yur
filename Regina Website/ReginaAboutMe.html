About Me

A Sociable Mechanical Engineer

Striving to merge my passions for technical design and helping people.

I have a strong desire to learn, which makes any field - no matter my level of experience - both enticing and attainable. I aim to always work harder, do more, and improve upon myself, so that I may be in the best position to help those who need it the most.

My personal interests - being primarily hiking and reading - tend to mimic my professional; in that I enjoy being on the go, moving quickly yet methodically, thinking through each step I make, while maintaining a sense of extrospection and understanding of the importance of adaptability.
